# Client Readiness Checklist (What to Prepare)

**Before we write code, the Client must act.**

## Phase 1: Business Readiness (Before Start)
- [ ] **Domain Name:** Purchased and accessible.
- [ ] **Legal Entity:** Registered (for Terms/Privacy).
- [ ] **Bank/Payment:** Stripe/PayPal account active (if selling).
- [ ] **Contact Points:** Dedicated email (support@...) and phone.

## Phase 2: Asset Readiness (Gate 2)
- [ ] **Logo:** Vector format (SVG/AI) + Dark/Light versions.
- [ ] **Images:** High-res photography (Not stock photos if possible).
- [ ] **Social Media:** Accounts created, handles claimed.

## Phase 3: Content Readiness
- [ ] **Pricing:** Final list (including tax/shipping logic).
- [ ] **Services:** Definitions of what is sold.
- [ ] **About:** The story/bio (Draft is fine, AI will polish).
- [ ] **Policies:** Refund/Cancellation rules.

## Phase 4: Operational Readiness (Before Launch)
- [ ] **Person in Charge:** Who answers the support email?
- [ ] **Order/Booking Flow:** Who fulfills the request?
- [ ] **Notification Email:** Where do system alerts go?

## Phase 5: Future Growth (After Launch)
- [ ] **Marketing Budget:** How will people find the site?
- [ ] **Content Plan:** Who writes the next article?

**Rule:** If Phase 1 & 2 are missing, the project is **Paused**.
