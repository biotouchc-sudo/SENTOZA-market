### 1. Brand "Soul" & Identity
*   **Context**: Defining the core "Vibe", "Mission", or "Visual Signature".
*   **Why**: AI averages existing data. "Soul" comes from unique deviation, not average.
*   **Role**: AI suggests 10 options; Human curates/mixes.

### 2. Crisis Communication
*   **Context**: Apologizing for a major service outage or data breach.
*   **Why**: AI lacks genuine empathy. "Perfect" corporate apologies sound robotic and offensive.
*   **Role**: Human writes from the heart; AI checks for grammar/clarity only.

### 3. Ethical Trade-offs
*   **Context**: "Should we sell user data to survive?" "Should we block this user?"
*   **Why**: These are moral choices, not optimization problems.
*   **Role**: AI provides risk analysis; Human takes moral responsibility.

### 4. High-Stakes Legal/Medical Advice
*   **Context**: TOS generation, Privacy Policy specific clauses, Diagnosis.
*   **Why**: Liability. Hallucination risk is non-zero.
*   **Role**: AI templates; Lawyer verifies.
