# Value Scorecard

**Pass Mark:** 7/10 required to build.

## Scoring Criteria (0-2 points each)

1. **Conversion Impact**
   - 0: No impact
   - 1: Indirect impact (brand)
   - 2: Direct impact (checkout/signup)

2. **Retention Impact**
   - 0: One-time use
   - 1: Monthly value
   - 2: Daily habit forming

3. **Cost Efficiency**
   - 0: Increases cost significantly
   - 1: Neutral
   - 2: Reduces cost/complexity

4. **Strategic Moat**
   - 0: Standard feature
   - 1: Hard to copy
   - 2: Unique IP / Data advantage

5. **User Pain Relief**
   - 0: "Nice to have"
   - 1: Solves annoyance
   - 2: Solves critical blocker
